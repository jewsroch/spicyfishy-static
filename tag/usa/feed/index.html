Our trip to the USA was amazing.  Thanks to our Families and Friends for being flexible and seeing us whenever it was possible.  We were so happy to see everyone and so thankful that we were able to spend so much time with so many of you.  We also were pretty happy for the food too

Hopefully we’ll have some new posts up very soon with more substance about the changes we saw and reverse/reversereverse culture-shock.  We’ll also be posting about what’s next and our plans for these 6 months.  (aka save money, then go to china and nepal on our way home…)
